What exactly is the pelvic floor and why should men care?

The pelvic floor is a group of muscles at the base of the pelvis that support your bladder, bowel, and sexual function. In men, a strong pelvic floor improves bladder control, erection quality, ejaculatory control, and core stability. Weakness in these muscles is more common than most men realize ‚Äî and almost always treatable with the right exercises.

Is this the same as Kegel exercises?

Kegels are one type of pelvic floor exercise, but ZenPhysic goes much further. Our programs include coordination training, strength progressions, breathing patterns, and relaxation techniques ‚Äî all calibrated to your goals. Over-doing Kegels without proper guidance can actually cause tension issues, which our approach helps prevent.
